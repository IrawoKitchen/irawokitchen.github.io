Our Story

"Irawo” means star in the Yoruba Language. A thing or person that can not be shut by darkness. That is what we believe food to be.

We marry tasty seeds & beans native to West Africa with award-winning Irish meat & vegetables to create delicious Afro-Fusion dishes.

Our interpretation of ‘Afro-Fusion’ is the combination of traditional dishes from various ethnic groups on the continent, blended with ‘healthy-ish’ ingredients creating memorable tastes through Anita’s individual expression.

Irawo Kitchen has set itself on a mission of introducing everyone in Ireland to the exciting ‘Afro-Fusion’ dishes in a professional, well-packaged delivery medium.
